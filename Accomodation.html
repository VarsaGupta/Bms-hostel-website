THE BMSETH HOSTELS

HOSTEL UNIT-I : MAIN HOSTEL

The Indian Main Hostel for male students was built in 1977 with a plinth area of 39,000 sq. ft. The hostel has a ground plus two floors and a small basement, and it can accommodate 150 pre-final and final year male students in single rooms.

GIRLS HOSTEL BLOCK UNIT-II

The block constructed between 1980-1984 accommodates female students with a plinth area of 42,586 sq. feet. The hostel has Ground plus 5 floors with 125 fully furnished rooms.The hostel is managed by the International Co-operation division.

Hostel Unit-III: BOYS HOSTEL

A unit constructed in 2003 accommodates 288 boys with a plinth area of 5,611.29 sq meters (60,377.48 sq. ft.) including a basement, ground, and five upper floors. Each floor consists of three wings with 8 twin-sharing rooms per wing.

Hostel Unit-IV: GIRLS HOSTEL

A hostel building constructed in 2003 is exclusively for 117 girl students with a plinth area of 5402 sq. meters (5,8125 sq. ft.). The hostel has a basement, ground, and three upper floors, with three students accommodated in each room.

Main Hostel (Phase-1) (GIRLS HOSTEL)

This newly constructed hostel is also exclusively for girl students. This new hostel building with a plinth area of 539 sq meters (5,799.64 sq. ft.) comprising of basement, ground and three upper floors was completed in the year 2012 to accommodate 76 girls.

Multi-Purpose Hall

This is used for conducting the meetings, seminars, functions organized by the management, cultural programs, farewell functions, get-togethers,etc. This hall is equipped with a stage and can accommodate about 200 peoples, attached with a green room, required furniture and audio systems.

ACCOMODATION FACILITIES

HOSTEL ROOMS

All rooms are fully furnished.Each student is provided with a wardrobe, study table, chair, cot and book storing cabinet.

HOSTEL WASHROOMS

The hostel washrooms are clean and hygenic.
